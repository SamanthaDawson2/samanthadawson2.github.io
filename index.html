Recruitment Done Right

Sage Recruiting was founded on the notion that recruitment can be done better. We are a team of highly technical recruiters who are passionate about helping clients and candidates achieve their goals, and the innovations created from emerging technologies. We take a very different approach to recruitment; we focus communication directly on the candidate allowing us to reach ‘the unreachable’, and listen closely to the wants and needs of our clients in order to achieve optimal results, every time.

Monthly Blog

February 2017: LA, California

I took a trip to California on Family Day weekend and boy was it fun! My flight was delayed 4+ hours because of a torrential downpour but other than that it was a perfect weekend! We walked along Venice Beach to Santa Monica Pier, hiked through the Hollywood Hills, checked out West Hollywood and ate a ton of food! I would recommend checking out Sugerfish in Santa Monica and Kings Road Cafe on Beverly Blvd.

March 2017: Women in Technology

Sage in Tech and Ecentricarts hosted an event to celebrate and discuss how far women in the technology field have come on March 8th. There were five speakers rangings from COO, Founders, CEO, Principle Engineers and Research Scientiest who took the floor and discussed issues women in tech have over come and issues we are still working on. It was an empowering event to be at! Sage will be hosting more meetups in the future, and we would love to see you all there. I will keep you posted on our next meet up!
